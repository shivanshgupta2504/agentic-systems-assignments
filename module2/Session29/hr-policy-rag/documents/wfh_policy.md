# InnoTech Solutions — Work From Home (WFH) Policy
**Version:** 1.4 | **Effective Date:** March 1, 2025 | **Owner:** HR Department

---

## 1. Purpose

InnoTech Solutions recognises that flexible working arrangements can enhance productivity and employee satisfaction. This policy establishes clear guidelines for working from home to ensure business continuity, team collaboration, and accountability are maintained.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all permanent full-time employees who have completed their probation period. It does not apply to employees on contract, interns, or those in roles designated as on-site mandatory (e.g., IT infrastructure, facilities).

---

## 3. WFH Eligibility

### 3.1 General Eligibility Criteria

An employee is eligible to work from home if they meet **all** of the following conditions:

- Has successfully completed the **6-month probation period**.
- Has a **performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or above** in the most recent appraisal cycle.
- Has a **reliable internet connection** (minimum 25 Mbps) at their remote location.
- Has a **dedicated and distraction-free workspace** at home.
- Their role does not require mandatory physical presence (verified by the department head).

### 3.2 Roles Ineligible for WFH

The following roles are classified as on-site mandatory and are **not eligible** for WFH:

- IT Infrastructure & Network Operations
- Facilities and Office Administration
- Any role explicitly marked "On-Site Required" in the employment contract

---

## 4. WFH Entitlement

| Employee Category          | WFH Days Allowed Per Week |
|----------------------------|---------------------------|
| Post-probation employees   | Up to 2 days              |
| Senior staff (5+ years)    | Up to 3 days              |
| Employees with disability  | Up to 4 days (case basis) |

- WFH days **cannot be accumulated** and do not carry over to the following week.
- WFH cannot be combined with half-day leaves on the same day unless approved by HR.
- A minimum of **3 days per week in-office** is mandatory for all eligible employees.

---

## 5. Approval Process

### 5.1 Recurring WFH Schedule

- Employees seeking a **fixed weekly WFH day** (e.g., every Wednesday WFH) must apply through the HR Portal under **"Flexible Work Arrangement Request"**.
- The request is reviewed by the reporting manager and approved or declined within **5 working days**.
- Once approved, the fixed schedule remains valid for **one quarter** and must be renewed.

### 5.2 Ad-hoc WFH Requests

- Ad-hoc WFH requests must be submitted **by 9:00 PM the previous evening** via the HR Portal or by notifying the manager directly.
- Same-day WFH requests (e.g., due to illness or emergency) must be communicated **before 9:30 AM** on that day via WhatsApp or email to the reporting manager, followed by a portal entry.
- **More than 3 unapproved same-day WFH instances per quarter** will result in a formal review.

---

## 6. Employee Responsibilities While on WFH

All employees working from home are expected to:

- Be **available during standard working hours** (10:00 AM – 7:00 PM IST or as per their shift).
- Respond to messages, emails, and calls **within 30 minutes** during core hours (11:00 AM – 5:00 PM).
- Attend all **scheduled meetings via video** unless the meeting host permits audio-only.
- Maintain their **camera on** during team standups and client calls unless explicitly excused.
- Log their daily work summary on the project tracking tool by **7:30 PM** on WFH days.
- Ensure that confidential company data is **not accessed from public networks** (e.g., cafes, airports) without using the company VPN.

---

## 7. Equipment and Security

- The company provides a **laptop and necessary peripherals**. Employees are responsible for their safe use at home.
- Employees must connect to the **InnoTech VPN** before accessing internal systems, repositories, or HR tools.
- Any security incident (e.g., lost laptop, phishing email clicked) must be reported to IT Support **immediately** via security@innotech.com.
- Employees must not use **personal devices** for official work involving sensitive data without prior IT approval.

---

## 8. Performance and Accountability

- WFH privileges are subject to periodic review by managers.
- Consistent failure to meet deliverables or maintain availability during WFH may result in **suspension of WFH privileges for one quarter**.
- If WFH privileges are suspended, the employee must complete a **30-day in-office period** before reapplying.

---

## 9. Policy Abuse

The following behaviours are considered misuse of the WFH policy and may lead to disciplinary action:

- Marking oneself as "available" while not actively working.
- Sharing company devices or credentials with others at the remote location.
- Conducting personal business activities during WFH hours.
- Repeatedly missing standups or client calls without prior notice.

---

## 10. Review and Amendments

This policy is reviewed **every 6 months**. Employees will be notified of updates via email and the HR Portal. Continued use of WFH facilities constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
