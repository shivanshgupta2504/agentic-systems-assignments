# InnoTech Solutions — Code of Conduct
**Version:** 2.3 | **Effective Date:** January 1, 2025 | **Owner:** HR & Legal Department

---

## 1. Purpose

InnoTech Solutions is committed to maintaining a workplace that is ethical, respectful, and inclusive. This Code of Conduct defines the standards of behaviour expected from every employee, contractor, and intern. Adherence to this code is a condition of continued employment at InnoTech Solutions.

---

## 2. Scope

This Code applies to all individuals engaged with InnoTech Solutions, including:
- Full-time and part-time employees
- Contractors and consultants
- Interns and apprentices
- Visitors with access to company systems or premises

It applies to behaviour on company premises, during work-related travel, at company events, and on digital channels (email, Slack, GitHub, etc.) used for official communication.

---

## 3. Workplace Behaviour Standards

### 3.1 Respect and Inclusion

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with **dignity and respect**, regardless of:

- Gender, gender identity, or sexual orientation
- Race, ethnicity, nationality, or religion
- Age, disability, or socioeconomic background
- Designation, team, or tenure

InnoTech Solutions has a **zero-tolerance policy** for harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind. This includes:

- Verbal abuse, offensive jokes, or derogatory remarks
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Excluding individuals from meetings or conversations based on protected characteristics
- Online harassment via internal or external communication tools

Employees who witness such behaviour are encouraged to report it immediately.

### 3.2 Professional Conduct

Employees are expected to:

- Arrive on time for work, meetings, and client engagements.
- Meet deadlines and communicate proactively if timelines are at risk.
- Maintain a professional tone in all written and verbal communication.
- Dress appropriately for their role and work environment (business casual on-site; smart casual for client visits).
- Refrain from using offensive, discriminatory, or abusive language in any setting.

### 3.3 Violence and Threatening Behaviour

Any act or threat of physical violence, intimidation, or threatening behaviour on company premises or during company events will result in **immediate termination** and may be referred to law enforcement.

---

## 4. Data Privacy and Confidentiality

### 4.1 Company Data

Employees have access to confidential company information as part of their role. This includes but is not limited to:
- Business plans and financial data
- Source code, product designs, and roadmaps
- Customer lists, contracts, and pricing
- Internal communications

Employees must:
- Access only the data necessary for their role.
- **Not share confidential data** with unauthorised individuals inside or outside the company.
- Store confidential documents in approved platforms (Google Drive, company servers) and not on personal devices.
- **Permanently delete** company data from personal devices upon offboarding.

### 4.2 Personal Data (DPDP Compliance)

InnoTech Solutions is committed to compliance with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023**. Employees handling personal data of customers or colleagues must:

- Collect and process personal data **only for legitimate business purposes**.
- Never share customer personal data with third parties without explicit approval from the Legal team.
- Report any suspected **data breach** to the IT Security team within **1 hour** of discovery.
- Complete the mandatory **Data Privacy Training** assigned on the Learning Portal.

### 4.3 Post-Employment Obligations

Confidentiality obligations extend **beyond the period of employment**. Employees must not use or disclose company confidential information after separation, as governed by the Non-Disclosure Agreement signed at onboarding.

---

## 5. Conflict of Interest

### 5.1 What Constitutes a Conflict of Interest

A conflict of interest arises when an employee's personal interests interfere — or appear to interfere — with InnoTech Solutions' interests. Examples include:

- Holding a **financial interest or employment** in a competitor, supplier, or customer.
- Using company resources to run a personal business.
- Hiring or influencing the hiring of a **family member or close associate**.
- Accepting **gifts or hospitality** from vendors in excess of ₹3,000 in value.
- Making business decisions that financially benefit the employee or their relatives.

### 5.2 Disclosure Obligation

Employees who identify or suspect a conflict of interest (real or potential) must **disclose it in writing** to their manager and HR within **5 working days** of becoming aware.

Failure to disclose a known conflict of interest is a serious violation and may result in disciplinary action including termination.

### 5.3 Secondary Employment

Employees wishing to take up **freelance, consulting, or part-time employment** outside InnoTech Solutions must obtain prior written approval from HR. Such activities must not:
- Involve a competitor, client, or vendor of InnoTech Solutions.
- Be conducted during InnoTech Solutions' working hours.
- Utilise InnoTech Solutions' resources, tools, or proprietary information.

---

## 6. Use of Company Resources

### 6.1 Acceptable Use

Company-provided resources (laptops, software licenses, internet access, meeting rooms) are provided for **official business use**. Limited personal use is acceptable as long as it does not interfere with work or violate any other section of this Code.

### 6.2 Prohibited Use

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Downloading, storing, or distributing **illegal, pirated, or adult content** on company devices.
- Using company equipment for **personal commercial activities** or external businesses.
- Installing **unauthorised software** on company devices without IT approval.
- Sharing **VPN credentials or access tokens** with any external party.
- Using company infrastructure to **mine cryptocurrency** or run personal servers.

---

## 7. Social Media and Public Communication

- Employees must not make public statements (on social media or otherwise) that **misrepresent InnoTech Solutions**, its products, clients, or employees.
- Employees must not share **confidential company information, financial data, or client names** on personal social media profiles.
- If approached by a journalist or media outlet about the company, employees must **redirect to the Communications team** at communications@innotech.com.
- Employees are free to share pride in their work (e.g., product launches) as long as no confidential information is disclosed.

---

## 8. Reporting Violations

InnoTech Solutions provides multiple channels for reporting potential violations of this Code:

| Channel                  | Contact                           |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Direct Manager           | For day-to-day behavioural issues |
| HR Business Partner      | hr@innotech.com                   |
| Ethics Hotline (Anonymous) | ethics@innotech.com             |
| Legal Team               | legal@innotech.com                |

- All reports are treated with **strict confidentiality**.
- **Retaliation against any employee** who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited and is itself a violation of this Code.
- Anonymous reports are accepted and investigated with the same rigour as identified ones.

---

## 9. Disciplinary Action

Violations of this Code will be reviewed by HR and the relevant manager. Depending on the severity, consequences may include:

| Severity     | Examples                                       | Consequence                        |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Minor        | Tardiness, casual language, minor policy slip  | Verbal warning, counselling        |
| Moderate     | Repeated misconduct, insubordination           | Written warning, PIP               |
| Serious      | Data misuse, harassment, conflict of interest  | Suspension or termination          |
| Gross        | Violence, fraud, theft, gross data breach      | Immediate termination + legal action |

All disciplinary proceedings follow a **fair and documented process** in accordance with applicable labour laws.

## 10. Acknowledgement

Upon joining InnoTech Solutions, every employee is required to read and sign the **Code of Conduct Acknowledgement Form**, confirming they have understood and agree to abide by this policy. This form is stored in the employee's HR file.

This Code is reviewed **annually** and updated as required. Employees will be notified of significant changes.
