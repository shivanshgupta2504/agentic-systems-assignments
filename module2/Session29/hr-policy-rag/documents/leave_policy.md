# InnoTech Solutions — Leave Policy
**Version:** 2.1 | **Effective Date:** January 1, 2025 | **Owner:** HR Department

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the leave entitlements and procedures for all full-time employees at InnoTech Solutions. It ensures consistent, fair treatment across teams while supporting employee well-being.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all permanent full-time employees. Contractors, interns, and part-time employees are subject to separate leave provisions defined in their respective agreements.

---

## 3. Types of Leave

### 3.1 Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)

- All full-time employees are entitled to **18 days of annual leave** per calendar year.
- Leave accrues at a rate of **1.5 days per month** starting from the date of joining.
- New employees are eligible to take accrued leave after completing **3 months** of service.
- Annual leave must be applied for at least **5 working days in advance** and approved by the reporting manager.

### 3.2 Sick Leave

- Employees are entitled to **10 days of sick leave** per calendar year.
- Sick leave does not accrue and is credited in full at the beginning of each year (January 1st).
- For absences exceeding **2 consecutive days**, a medical certificate from a registered practitioner must be submitted to HR within 3 working days of return.
- Sick leave cannot be encashed or carried forward.

### 3.3 Casual Leave

- Employees are entitled to **6 days of casual leave** per calendar year.
- Casual leave is meant for urgent or unforeseen personal matters.
- No more than **2 consecutive casual leave days** may be taken at a time.
- Casual leave must be applied for on the same day or one day prior when possible.

### 3.4 Maternity Leave

- Female employees are entitled to **26 weeks (182 days)** of paid maternity leave as per the Maternity Benefit Act.
- Applicable after completing at least **80 days of employment** in the 12 months preceding the expected delivery date.
- An additional **4 weeks of unpaid leave** may be granted upon request, subject to manager and HR approval.

### 3.5 Paternity Leave

- Male employees are entitled to **7 days of paid paternity leave** within 3 months of the child's birth or adoption.
- This leave must be applied for at least **2 weeks in advance** (except in case of early or emergency delivery).

### 3.6 Bereavement Leave

- Employees may take up to **5 days of paid bereavement leave** in the event of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling).
- Up to **3 days** are granted for the death of an extended family member (grandparent, in-law).
- Supporting documentation may be requested by HR.

### 3.7 Public Holidays

- InnoTech Solutions observes **12 mandatory public holidays** per year as declared by the government.
- A list of applicable holidays is published on the internal HR portal at the start of each year.
- Employees required to work on public holidays will receive a **compensatory day off** to be used within 30 days.

---

## 4. Carry-Forward Rules

| Leave Type     | Carry-Forward Allowed | Maximum Carry-Forward |
|----------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|
| Annual Leave   | Yes                   | Up to 9 days          |
| Sick Leave     | No                    | —                     |
| Casual Leave   | No                    | —                     |
| Maternity      | N/A                   | —                     |
| Paternity      | No                    | —                     |

- Carried-forward annual leave must be utilised by **March 31** of the following year.
- Any unused carry-forward balance after March 31 will **lapse without encashment**.

---

## 5. Leave Encashment

- A maximum of **6 days of annual leave** may be encashed once per calendar year.
- Leave encashment is processed during the **December payroll cycle**.
- Encashment is calculated based on the employee's basic daily salary.
- Employees who resign or are separated may encash all remaining annual leave balance as part of their full and final settlement.

---

## 6. Leave Application Process

1. Log into the **InnoTech HR Portal** (hr.innotech.internal).
2. Navigate to **Leave Management → Apply for Leave**.
3. Select the leave type, dates, and add a brief reason.
4. Submit for manager approval.
5. Upon approval, an automated confirmation email is sent to both the employee and manager.

> **Note:** Leaves taken without prior approval (except genuine emergencies) may be treated as Leave Without Pay (LWP).

---

## 7. Leave Without Pay (LWP)

- Employees who exhaust their leave balance and require additional time off may apply for LWP.
- LWP requires **written approval from both the reporting manager and HR**.
- LWP will result in a proportional salary deduction for the period.
- Excessive or unauthorised LWP may affect performance evaluations.

---

## 8. Policy Violations

Misuse of leave (e.g., falsifying medical certificates, taking leave under false pretences) will be treated as a disciplinary matter and may result in corrective action including termination.
